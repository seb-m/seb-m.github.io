pub fn bytes_cswap<T: Signed + Primitive + Int>(cond: T, x: &mut [T], y: &mut [T])

Conditionally swap bytes.

x and y are swapped iff cond is equal to 1, there are left unchanged iff cond is equal to 0. Currently only works for arrays of signed integers. cond is expected to be 0 or 1.
